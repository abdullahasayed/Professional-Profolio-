Field notes

Every plate above is a multi-frame Instagram post — covers designed with typographic species titles, followed by the clean frames. The habit started at Boston College, around the Arnold Arboretum and Chestnut Hill Reservoir; the territory moved with me to the preserves of North Texas. Same patience either way.

Follow along at @abdullahsayedphotography, or the everything-else account, @mster_sayed.
